📋 Workshop Planning Checklist

Click on any item to mark it as completed!

👥 1. Small Group Leaders (10)

  • Recruit 10 committed leaders skilled in worship, music, and youth discipleship.
  • Assign each a specific small group (balance by age/gender).
  • Brief on goals: spiritual growth, TAWG, music practice.
  • Daily check-ins for devotions, attendance, oversight.
  • Provide teaching materials for sessions.

🏠 2. Dorm Caretakers (Boys & Girls)

  • Appoint 4 male for boys' dorm + 4 female for girls' (responsible early risers).
  • Train on 6AM wake-up, devotions, duties.
  • Oversight: Breakfast attendance, 11PM lights-out, spiritual accountability.
  • Daily: Room checks, prayer times, conflict resolution.

🤝 3. Ministry Support Teams

  • Dining Team: 4-6 for food prep, serving, hygiene, cleanup 🍲
  • Discipline Team: 3-4 to enforce rules, monitor youth ⚖️
  • Mobile Team: 2-3 for lockers, timed access 📱
  • Errands Team: 2-3 for trips/supplies 🚗
  • Menu Team: 10-day balanced menu + backups 📝
  • First Aid: 2 trained + kit/hospital contacts 🏥
  • Transport: 2-3 vehicles ready 🚙
  • Food Service: Extra serving hands
  • Cleanup: Delegates + 4 volunteers 🧹

🚪 4. Welcome & Registration Team

  • Reception
  • Registration: Forms, payments, allotments
  • Room allotment
  • Registration kits

🗺️ 5. Venue Assistance & Logistics

  • Guide to venue
  • Transport options
  • Support arrival/departure

🙌 6. Ministry Team

  • Prayer times availability
  • Altar calls
  • Small group devotions
  • Guest worship teams (Hyd)
  • Pastors for ministry
